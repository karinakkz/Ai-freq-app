Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 4, 2026

AI Freq's ("we", "our", or "us") operates the AI Freq's mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our App.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we may collect your email address.
  • Voice Data: When you use voice commands, your voice is temporarily processed to convert speech to text. We use OpenAI's Whisper service for this processing.
  • Task Data: Tasks, reminders, and notes you create within the App.
  • Payment Information: When you make purchases, payment processing is handled securely by Stripe. We do not store your credit card details.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: How you interact with the App, including features used and frequency sessions played.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and app version.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve the App's functionality
  • Process your voice commands using AI
  • Create and manage your tasks and reminders
  • Process payments for premium features
  • Send important updates about the App
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve user experience

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Your data is stored securely on cloud servers
  • We use encryption for data transmission
  • Voice recordings are processed in real-time and not permanently stored
  • We implement industry-standard security measures

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Delete: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Opt-out: Disable certain data collection features
  • Export: Request your data in a portable format

To exercise these rights, contact us at: support@aifreqs.app

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us immediately.

7. Data Retention

  • Account data is retained while your account is active
  • Voice data is processed in real-time and not stored permanently
  • You can delete your data at any time through the App settings

8. International Users

If you are accessing the App from outside Australia, please be aware that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in Australia or other countries where our servers are located.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the App and updating the "Last Updated" date.
