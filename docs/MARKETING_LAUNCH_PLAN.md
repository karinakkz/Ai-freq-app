## ⭐ Review Strategy (Critical for App Store!)

### How to Get 5-Star Reviews:

1. **Friends & Family First**
   - Ask 10-20 people to download and review
   - Guide them: "If you love it, I'd appreciate a 5-star review!"

2. **In-App Review Prompt**
   - Trigger after positive actions (completing a frequency session)
   - Use `expo-store-review` for native prompt
   - Don't prompt too early or too often

3. **Respond to All Reviews**
   - Thank positive reviewers
   - Address negative feedback professionally
   - Shows you're an active developer

### Target: 10+ Reviews in First Week
- This helps App Store algorithm visibility
- Average rating should be 4.5+ stars
