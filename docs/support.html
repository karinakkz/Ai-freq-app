💬 Frequently Asked Questions

How do I use voice commands?

Tap the microphone button on the home screen and speak naturally. For example, say "Create a task to buy groceries tomorrow" or "What's on my schedule today?"

What are frequency sessions?

Frequency sessions are audio tracks designed for relaxation and wellness. Browse the Frequency Library to explore different categories like Stress Relief, Focus, and Sleep.

How do I purchase premium packs?

Go to the Premium Packs section from the home screen. You can purchase individual packs ($4.99 each) or unlock everything with the Lifetime package ($49).

Can I get a refund?

Refund requests are considered on a case-by-case basis. Contact us at support@aifreqs.app within 14 days of your purchase.

Is my data secure?

Yes! We use encryption for all data transmission. Voice recordings are processed in real-time and not permanently stored. See our Privacy Policy for details.

The app isn't working correctly

Try these steps: 1) Force close and reopen the app, 2) Check your internet connection, 3) Update to the latest version. If issues persist, contact us with details about your device and the problem.
